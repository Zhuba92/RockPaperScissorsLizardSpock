Rock * Paper * Scissors * Lizard * Spock

The Rules Are As Follows:

  • Lizard ---> Beats: Spock and Paper, but loses to: Scissors and Rock
  • Spock ---> Beats: Scissors and Rock, but loses to: Paper and Lizard
